Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

At BudgetBee, we prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Data: When you register an account, we may collect your name, email address, and authentication credentials.

Financial Data: Any income, expenses, categories, or budget data you input is stored locally on your device or securely synced to our encrypted cloud servers, depending on your settings.

Usage Data: We automatically collect diagnostic data such as app crash logs, device model, operating system version, and general app usage statistics to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the BudgetBee application.
  • To synchronize your data across multiple devices (if enabled).
  • To send you important notifications regarding your account or security updates.
  • To respond to your customer support requests.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard encryption (AES-256) to protect your financial data. We do not sell your personal or financial data to third parties. Data in transit is secured using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) for crash reporting and anonymous analytics. These services have their own privacy policies governing data usage.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, modify, or permanently delete your personal data. You can initiate an account deletion directly from the Delete Account page or within the mobile app settings.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
