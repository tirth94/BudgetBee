Privacy Policy

App Name: BudgetBee (com.mthreecreatives.budgetbee)

Developer: M-Three Creatives

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

1. Introduction

M-Three Creatives ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Android mobile application, BudgetBee ("the App").

BudgetBee is a personal finance and expense tracking application designed to help you securely manage your personal financial records, track income and expenses, and categorize your transactions.

By downloading, accessing, or using BudgetBee, you agree to the collection and use of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with our core personal finance tracking features, we may collect the following types of information:

Application Data:
  • Income and expenses
  • Custom categories created by you
  • Notes attached to transactions
  • User preferences and settings
  • Feedback submitted directly through the App's Contact page

What we DO NOT collect:

  • Phone numbers, SMS, or contacts
  • Camera photos, gallery images, or audio recordings
  • Device location data (GPS)
  • Calendar data or health information
  • Payment card information or bank account credentials

3. Google Sign-In

BudgetBee supports authentication exclusively via Google Sign-In. When you choose to log in using your Google account, we collect the following basic profile information provided by Google:

  • Your Name
  • Your Email Address
  • Your Profile Picture

We do not support email/password login, phone number login, or anonymous login procedures.

4. Guest Mode

BudgetBee offers a Guest Mode for users who prefer not to authenticate with a Google account. While using Guest Mode, your financial data is strictly stored locally on your device. However, certain cloud synchronization features will not be available until you authenticate.

5. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and operate the BudgetBee application.
  • To authenticate your identity using Google Sign-In.
  • To securely synchronize your personal financial data across your devices.
  • To process and review feedback submitted through the App.

6. Offline Storage & Cloud Sync

BudgetBee fully supports offline usage. If you add, edit, or delete financial records without an active internet connection, your data is securely stored locally on your device.

Once your device regains internet connectivity, the application automatically and securely synchronizes your local changes with our cloud infrastructure (Cloud Firestore) to ensure your data remains backed up and accessible across your authenticated devices.

7. Feedback

Users have the option to submit feedback or support requests from inside the BudgetBee application. Any information you voluntarily provide in these feedback submissions is securely transmitted and stored in Cloud Firestore for our development team to review and act upon.

8. Third-Party Services

To operate our application effectively, BudgetBee integrates with specific third-party services provided by Google. We use ONLY the following services:

  • Google Sign-In: For secure user authentication.
  • Firebase Authentication: To manage secure user sessions and identities.
  • Cloud Firestore: A NoSQL cloud database used to securely store and synchronize your financial data, categories, notes, preferences, and feedback.

We do not use or integrate Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Performance Monitoring, or Google AdMob. The current version of BudgetBee does not display advertisements, track analytics, or send push notifications.

9. Data Security

The security of your financial data is our highest priority. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information, including:

  • Secure Communication: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is secured using HTTPS protocols.
  • Firebase Security Rules: We utilize strict Cloud Firestore Security Rules to ensure that your data can only be accessed and modified by your authenticated account.
  • Authentication: Leveraging Firebase Authentication ensures robust, standardized security for user sessions.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

BudgetBee is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will immediately delete this data from our servers.

11. International Users

BudgetBee may be used worldwide. By accessing the application and providing your information, you acknowledge that your data will be processed and stored on Google’s secure cloud infrastructure, which may involve the transfer of data to jurisdictions outside your country of residence.

12. Data Retention

We retain your personal information and financial data only for as long as your BudgetBee account remains active. Once you request account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our active database systems.

13. Account & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can permanently delete your BudgetBee account and all associated financial records directly from within the application.

Upon triggering the deletion process, your authentication profile and all related Cloud Firestore documents (income, expenses, categories, notes) are immediately and permanently erased from our servers. This action cannot be undone.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately after they are posted.
